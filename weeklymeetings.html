weekly meetings

Charlotte Storytellers meets every Monday at 7:30 at Industry Coworking at the NC Music Factory.
We warm up with vocal exercises, physical movements, and storytelling games.
The lion's share of the meeting is spent workshopping stories on that week's theme.
Members receive critique on content, tone and performance.

If you're interested in coming to our meetings, find us on Facebook or Meetup.
