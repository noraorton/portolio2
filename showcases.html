story showcases

Every four months, we choose a few of our favorite stories and build a themed showcase around them.
Storytellers rehearse for a month and perform memorized, polished pieces.
Our shows have been at SEEDS Theater, The Frock Shop and 7th Street Public Market.
The most recent showcase had 200 attendees.

If you're interested in attending our shows, find us on Facebook or Meetup.
